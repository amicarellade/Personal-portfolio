About Me

When I'm not immersed in coding or tackling data science projects, you'll often find me enjoying my time outside. Whether it's waking up early to be one of the first skiers on the mountain to spending hours on the range practicing my golf swing, I always adore spending time outside.

I love to explore and visit new places when I am given the opportunity. Some notable locations outside of the US that I have been to include Doha, Auckland, Queenstown, Cairns, Melbourne, Hobart, London, and Cayman Islands. Each place has enriched my perspective and left me with unforgettable experiences.

I've been a football fan since I can remember and have always supported Manchester United through the thick and the thin (the last decade). Nevertheless, my favorite player growing up was Xavi Hernandez, now coach of Barcelona. In recent times, I've cultivated a strong passion for reading, steadily expanding my digital library. The thrill of trying new things is something I cherish, and my latest venture involves experimenting with the art of baking.
